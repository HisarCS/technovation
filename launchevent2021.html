Launch Event 2021



We initiated this year's Launch Event with Merve Nazlıoğlu from Insider's energetic speech! As she talked about her rising journey in Turkey as a women tech-entrepreneur, our participants were exhilarated to start their journey of their own.

Delving in depth to Technovation, our alumni Selin, İpek, and Başak talked about their Technovation experiences and how it shaped their career. Participants were able to receive answers to their questions about Technovation specifically.

Into the project! by the Student Ambassador hosted Design Thinking & Ideation workshops, students chose one of the Sustainable Development Goals of the UN, and worked towards their problem. Diving into the problem more specifically at each step of the workshop, students came up with their start-ups at the end of the event. Each team pitched their application and received feedback from our Chapter Ambassadors.
