Empower girls to be leaders and problem-solvers

As the Technovation Turkey team, we've been attending the Technovation Girls Challenge and inspiring young girls to be a pioneer in the STEM and Entrepreneurship fields since 2013. More than 15 teams have participated ever since, and most of our alumni have worked in/studied on STEM fields after their Technovation journey. As a large team, in the 2021 season, we consist of 2 Chapter Ambassadors and 3 Student Ambassadors. We are planning on organizing a Launch Event for the 2021 season, and the registrations are about to open! Stay tuned!

LAUNCH EVENT TURKEY 2020

In February 2020, student ambassadors Debi Ahitov and Dafne Sarfati organized a Launch Event in Hisar Schools, where Technovation basics were introduced, 2 software developers from Getir were hosted, design-thinking workshops were launched, and presentations of teams' ideas were made.

WORLD PITCH 2018

In August 2018, Dafne, Debi, Irmak, and Nil were awarded to be finalist participants for their application, "Tap Tap Trees". Tap Tap Trees is an application that enchances awareness for deforestation. The application encouraged kids to play a mobile game and help plant trees via the organization ÇEKÜL. As a result of their efforts, they were awarded by a trip to Silicon Valley for the Technovation World Pitch experience.
